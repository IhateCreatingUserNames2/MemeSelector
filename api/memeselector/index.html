MemeVault AI (Local Privacy Edition)

Your memes are processed in your browser and your index is saved on your computer. Nothing is stored on the server.

Step 1: Index a New Folder

Only do this for folders you haven't indexed before. This will create an `index.json` file for you to save.

Step 2: Start a Search Session

A. Load your saved `index.json` file.

B. Select the matching local meme folder.
